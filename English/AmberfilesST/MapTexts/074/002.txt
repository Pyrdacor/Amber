WHEN YOU ENTER THIS ROOM A SMELL OF FOUL FLESH AND ROTTING VEGETABLES REACHES YOUR NOSE . THE FLOOR IS COVERED WITH BROKEN CUPS AND PLATES . IN THE MIDDLE OF THE ROOM A HALF-ORC STANDS AT AN OLD WOODEN TROUGH AND IS WASHING THE PLATES . WHEN HE HEARS THE NOISE YOU MAKE OPENING THE DOOR HE TURNS ROUND . HE DROPS THE PLATE AND FLEES FRIGHTENED INTO A CORNER OF THE ROOM .
